About us

50 Years Celebration Trusted by Families for 50 Years

Since 1975, The Sunshine House has been a trusted partner for families. Our founders — both public school teachers — couldn’t find the quality early education they wanted for their daughter, so they created their own. Today, we’ve grown to over 100 schools, all built on the same mission: high-quality care and education – every child, every family, every day.
